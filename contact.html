Contact FAQs

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my message?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For catering requests, we aim to respond within 2 business hours during our operating hours.

Do you take reservations?

We operate on a first-come, first-served basis for parties under 8 people. For larger groups, please contact us in advance and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Where can I park when visiting?

We have limited parking behind the restaurant, plus 2-hour street parking is available on Food Street and surrounding areas. A public parking garage is located one block away on Flavor Ave.

Do you have gift cards available?

Yes! Gift cards can be purchased in-store or online through our website. They never expire and can be used for both food and merchandise.

Are you wheelchair accessible?

Absolutely. Our restaurant is fully wheelchair accessible with ramps at all entrances and accessible restrooms. Please let us know if you need any special accommodations.

Can I host a private event at Grub n Chill?

We'd love to host your event! We offer full restaurant buyouts for private events after regular business hours. Contact our events team for more information and pricing.
