Our Story

From humble beginnings to serving the community with delicious fast food

Who We Are

Founded in 2023, Grub n Chill was born from a simple idea: to create a fast food experience that doesn't compromise on quality or flavor. Our founder, Jamie Smith, grew up loving classic fast food but always dreamed of elevating it with better ingredients and a more relaxed atmosphere.

What started as a small food truck has now grown into a beloved local spot where people come not just for the food, but for the welcoming vibe that makes everyone feel at home.

Our Philosophy

At Grub n Chill, we believe fast food doesn't have to mean low quality. We're committed to:

  • Using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible
  • Preparing food to order for maximum freshness
  • Creating a welcoming environment for all our guests
  • Continuously innovating our menu while keeping the classics
  • Giving back to the community that supports us
